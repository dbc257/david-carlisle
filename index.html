About Me

While there are many things that I could write about myself,

I am essentially like this symbolic circle that is called "Ensō" in Japanese.

Ensō (円相) is:

  • a Japanese word meaning "circle"
  • a manifestation of the moment
  • an expression of absolute enlightenment
  • a representation of our true and intimate self

People can find many other meanings for Ensō.

Like me, it is a paradox.

Ensō is both full and empty.
